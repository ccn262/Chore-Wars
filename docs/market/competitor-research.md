# Competitor Research

## Research goals

- Understand the chore and household management space
- Identify where existing products feel too heavy, too boring, or too complex
- Find opportunities for a more playful mobile-first experience

## What to compare

- Household chore apps
- Family task apps
- Shared-home coordination tools
- Gamified productivity apps

## Comparison dimensions

- Mobile usability
- Speed to complete a task
- Internationalisation readiness
- Reporting clarity
- Tone and brand feel
