# App Store Strategy

## Early plan

- Launch as a web-first product
- Keep the UI and navigation ready for a later native wrapper
- Prepare store-friendly naming, screenshots, and copy later
- Apple App Store and Google Play listing strategy should be planned

## Competitor categories

- Family chore apps
- Kids allowance apps
- Family organiser apps
- Habit/task gamification apps

## Competitor examples to track

- S'moresUp
- Greenlight
- GoHenry
- BusyKid
- Cozi
- ChoreBucks
- Other chore chart and family organiser apps discovered during later research

## Store-readiness concerns

- Clear value proposition
- Fast onboarding
- Strong mobile UI
- Internationalised metadata
- Screenshots should show one-tap chore completion, leaderboard, weekly winner, rewards or forfeits, and fairness dashboard
