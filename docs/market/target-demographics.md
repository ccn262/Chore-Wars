# Target Demographics

## Primary audiences

- Families managing chores and allowances
- Couples balancing shared responsibilities
- Flatmates dividing recurring tasks
- Shared homes with mixed schedules
- Students sharing a home
- Renters managing fair chores
- Young professionals living with others

## Competitor categories

- Family chore apps
- Kids allowance apps
- Family organiser apps
- Habit/task gamification apps

## Competitor examples to track

- S'moresUp
- Greenlight
- GoHenry
- BusyKid
- Cozi
- ChoreBucks
- Other chore chart and family organiser apps discovered during later research

## Product fit

- Need fast, simple coordination
- Need low-friction mobile use
- Prefer a playful but not childish tone
- Benefit from visible progress and fair reward handling
- Want a competition feel without a hard-edged admin vibe
