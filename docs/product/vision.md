# Vision

Chore Wars turns household chores into a playful, fast, and fair points game.

## Vision statement

Make it effortless for people who share a home to agree on chores, complete them quickly, and keep the household running without nagging.

## What success feels like

- A parent, partner, or flatmate opens the app and sees what matters immediately
- They tap a chore and award points in seconds
- The app feels light, cheerful, and a little bit funny
- Everyone understands progress without reading a lot of text

## Product principles

- Speed first: the core action must be nearly instant
- Mobile first: the app should feel like a phone app, not a website
- Shared-home friendly: the product must support multiple living arrangements
- Fair and visible: chores and rewards should be easy to understand
- International-ready: language, date, time, and money must be locale aware

## Non-goals for the first release

- Heavy admin dashboards
- Complex household accounting
- Desktop-first workflows
- Deep social network features
- Feature bloat that slows down chore completion
