# App Summary

Chore Wars is a chore tracking and rewards app for shared homes.

## One-line summary

Track chores, award points, and keep the home moving with a fast, colourful mobile experience.

## Primary users

- Families
- Couples
- Flatmates
- Shared homes
- International households

## Core loop

1. Open the home screen
2. See the next useful chore
3. Tap to complete or award points
4. Get a quick celebratory response
5. Return later to a visual summary of progress

## Why it exists

Most chore tools are too dull, too slow, or too desktop-like. Chore Wars should be simple enough to use in real life, in the hallway, in the kitchen, or while holding a phone with one hand.
