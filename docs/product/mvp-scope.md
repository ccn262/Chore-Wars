# MVP Scope

The MVP should prove one thing: a household can open the app, complete chores quickly, and feel motivated to keep using it.

## In scope

- Household setup
- Household members
- Chore list
- Quick tap completion flow
- Points assignment
- Basic progress and reporting views
- Simple notification or reminder groundwork
- Locale-aware display for dates, times, and numbers
- International-ready copy structure

## Out of scope for MVP

- Complex automation rules
- Advanced budgets or expense splitting
- Social feeds
- Deep custom analytics
- Native mobile apps on day one
- Heavy customization that slows the first-use path

## MVP success criteria

- The home screen clearly shows what to do next
- A chore can be completed with minimal taps
- Points are awarded immediately after completion
- Reports are easy to understand on a phone
- Copy and formatting are ready for multiple locales
